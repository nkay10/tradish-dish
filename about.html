Where we come from

Food is more than just sustenance. Some dishes have deep cultural roots and their preparation have turned into long-standing traditions. Bratwurst, curry, pizza, and phở have long become epitomes of their countries of origin.

tradish-dish believes that recipes have been passed down from generation to generation for a good reason. These dishes have become more than just food – these meals resemble friends and family and the moments you shared, tradition and culture, they evoke childhood memories soothing your homesickness or let you relive your exotic travel adventures, they mean good times, laughter, and comfort and above all, provide a way for us to connect with each other.

What we are all about

Having lived in 10 countries on 3 continents, we discovered that the local food substantially contributed to the great memories made in each and every place. However, it was not just about the food but the stories that came with the dish, which allowed us to truly immerse ourselves in the different cultures and experience heartfelt hospitality. It is our aim to tease out people’s favorite recipes along with their stories from every corner of the world. We want to share these little gems with everyone who has an appreciation for traditional food (or newly invented traditions) and is keen to get an authentic insight into the world’s local cuisines.
